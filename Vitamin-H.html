Vitamin H (biotin)

Biotin is the savior of bald people. It not only prevents hair loss
and light exposure on the top of the head, but also prevents gray hair
in teenagers, which is common among modern people.

It also plays an important role in maintaining skin health. As for its
effect in stabilizing the nervous system, it has not yet been proven,
but it does help depression and insomnia to a certain extent.

Vitamin H effects:

  1. Help fat metabolism
  2. Helps metabolize amino acids and carbohydrates.
  3. Promote the normal operation and growth of sweat glands, nervous
    tissue, bone marrow, male gonads, skin and hair, and reduce the
    symptoms of eczema and dermatitis.
  4. Prevent gray hair and hair loss, and help treat baldness
  5. Relieve muscle pain

Food origin:

Brown rice, wheat, strawberries, grapefruit, grapes, beer, liver,
eggs, lean meat, dairy.

Need crowd:

People who eat raw eggs and drink alcohol need to supplement biotin;
people who take antibiotics or sulfa drugs should take at least 25 μg
a day; men with thinning hair should take biotin to prevent hair loss.
During pregnancy, biotin will be significantly lost and should be
replenished reasonably under the guidance of a physician.

Deficiency disorder:

Hair loss, depression, skin diseases such as eczema, dermatitis,
etc., lipodystrophy, etc.
