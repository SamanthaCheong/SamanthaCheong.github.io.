Pancake Dumplings

Ingredient Details

Main Ingredients

  1. Egg-2
  2. Flour-Appropriate amount

Additional Ingredients

  1. Chives-Appropriate amount
  2. Cooked white sesame seeds-Appropriate amount
  3. Salt- Appropriate amount
  4. Pepper-Appropriate amount

Cooking Method

  1. Taste-Light
  2. Craftsmanship-fry
  3. Time consuming-ten minutes
  4. Difficulty-Simple

Pancake Dumplings Recipe Steps

Ingredients: eggs, flour, chives.
Add appropriate amount of salt and pepper to the flour and mix well
Add appropriate amount of water in batches, stirring while adding,
to form a thinner batter.
Wash the chives and cut into pieces.
Heat the non-stick pan for a while, then reduce the heat to low, brush
with a thin layer of oil, scoop in a spoonful of batter, turn the pan,
and spread the bottom of the pan as thinly as possible.
When the surface of the dough starts to solidify, crack in an egg.
Spread the eggs with a spoon to cover the pancake.
When the egg liquid is about to completely solidify, sprinkle an appropriate
amount of bamboo sesame seeds and chopped green onion, and fry until
cooked through. (If you fry the egg liquid until it is completely dry
and then sprinkle it, it will not stick, so you should sprinkle it before
the egg liquid is completely solidified.)
In the past, I used to make all kinds of breakfast pancakes with non-stick pans.
Many friends said that there is no non-stick pan, so I tried it this time and
made one with a common iron pan for cooking. It was just as good, but even
thinner. So you don’t necessarily have to have a non-stick pan.
After frying, turn it over and add fried dough sticks, pork floss, etc.,
your favorite food, roll it up and eat it, it tastes particularly delicious.
Sometimes I don't even roll up my body, so I just make breakfast pancakes
like this.
