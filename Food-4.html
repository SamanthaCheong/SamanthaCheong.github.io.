Brioche

Ingredient Details

Main Ingredients

  1. Steamed bun-two

Additional Ingredients

  1. Egg- Two
  2. Salt-Appropriate amount
  3. Oil-Appropriate amount

Cooking Method

  1. Taste-Salty and Fresh
  2. Craftsmanship-fry
  3. Time consuming-ten minutes
  4. Difficulty-Simple

Brioche Recipe Steps

Prepare materials.
Beat the eggs with a little salt; cut the steamed buns into about 1 cm pieces.
Soak the steamed bun slices in the egg liquid for a few seconds and coat
evenly with a layer of egg liquid.
Put an appropriate amount of oil in the pan and add the steamed bun slices.
Fry over medium-low heat until both sides are golden brown.
Eat it while it's hot.
