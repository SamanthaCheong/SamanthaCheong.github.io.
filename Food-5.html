Pumpkin millet porridge

Ingredient Details

Main Ingredients

  1. Pumpkin-150g
  2. Millet-100 g
  3. Sticky rice-100 g

Additional Ingredients

  1. water-Appropriate amount

Cooking Method

  1. Taste-Sweetness
  2. Cook-Craftsmanship
  3. Time Consuming-Three Quarters Of An Hour
  4. Difficulty-Ordinary

Pumpkin Millet Porridge Recipe Steps

Prepare 150 grams of pumpkin.
Peel and cut into small cubes.
100 grams of millet and 100 grams of glutinous rice.
After washing, mix and place in a bowl, soak in water for about 1 hour
(soaking is to save the time of cooking porridge. If you are in a hurry,
you can directly add water to cook porridge without soaking, but the
cooking time will be slightly longer. For example, soaked rice usually
needs to be cooked for 10 minutes, while unsoaked rice needs to be cooked
for 10-20 minutes).
Pour millet, glutinous rice, and pumpkin into the pressure cooker, and
add 5 times the amount of water.
Cover the pot, bring up the steam over high heat, then turn to low heat,
and press for another 10-15 minutes.
