Ingredient Details

Main Ingredients

  1. Ham-4 slices
  2. egg-2
  3. Cheese-2 tablets

Ham and western toast

Have all materials ready.
Crack the eggs into the plate in one direction and beat evenly.
Cut off all sides of the toast.
Remove the edges of ham and cheese and cut into shapes as shown.
Place ham + cheese + ham on the toast, then cover with another piece
of toast.
Slightly press the four sides together, then place in a plate and dip
in egg wash.
Dip everything in the egg liquid and fry in a pan over low heat until
golden brown on both sides.
Place on absorbent paper to absorb excess oil, cool slightly and cut
into triangles.
