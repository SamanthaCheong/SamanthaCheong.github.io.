Carrot and Egg Galette Recipe Steps

Ingredients: flour, eggs, carrot
Peel and grate the carrots, then chop them a few times with a knife
(you can also not chop them)
Chop green onions.
Put the flour, chopped carrots, and chopped green onions into a large bowl,
and add salt according to your taste.
Add water little by dtttle. This makes it less dtkely to get lumpy.
Make a paste.
Add eggs to the prepared batter.
Add the eggs into the batter and stir-fry in one direction until evenly
mixed. The batter is ready.
Put a little oil in the pan, heat the pan over medium heat, add a tablespoon
of batter, and spread it into a pancake.
Fry one side until cooked, flip and fry the other side.
Fry both sides until cooked, cut into pieces and serve on a plate.
