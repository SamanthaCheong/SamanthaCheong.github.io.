Direction of Use

Make NewLife™ Apple Cider Vinegar a part of your daily routine.
It can be consumed or used topically. Here are some tips on
how to use it:

For general wellness
Mix 1 teaspoon of Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV) with 1 tablespoon
of raw honey in a glass of distilled water and consume two or
more times daily. You can also use ACV to add some zest to
your salad and dishes.
For headaches, sinus, asthma and flu congestion
Boil a cup of distilled water and 2 tablespoons of ACV.
Take off the heat and inhale the vapor (put a towel over
your head to increase effectiveness).
Get that glow!
Chill equal parts ACV and distilled water and use as a
toner for your skin. Have a DIY facial at home and open up
your pores using vapor using your ACV water.
Halt the scalp problems
Soak a cotton ball in ACV and apply generously onto the
scalp. Leave in for at least 15 minutes before shampooing.
Relieves itchy and dry scalp and unwanted dandruff.
Combats sore throat
Add a tablespoon of ACV in half a glass of water and gargle
hourly. To maintain wellness, gargle once or twice a week to
remove toxins trapped in your throat.
