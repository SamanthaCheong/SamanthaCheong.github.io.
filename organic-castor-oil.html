Direction of Use

  1. Hair Tonic
    Ingredients:
    ½ cup of NewLife™ Pure Castor Oil
    Directions:
    1. Using a spray bottle, dampen your hair.
    2. Take some NewLife™ Pure Castor Oil in
    your palm and work it through your hair
    from the roots to the tips while massaging
    your scalp during the process.
    3. Leave the oil in for a minimum of 15-20 minutes.
    You could also leave it overnight.
    4. Follow up by shampooing and conditioning as normal.
  2. 2-in-1 Cleanser & Moisturizer for Oily & Acne-Prone Skin
    Ingredients:
    To make 100ml of 2-in-1 Cleanser & Moisturizer
    1. 15ml of NewLife™ Pure Castor Oil
    2. 85ml of NewLife™ Extra Virgin Coconut Oil
    Directions:
    1. Mix the two ingredients.
    2. Warm up the face with a warm towel to open up
    the pores.
    3. Apply a few drops of the oil on the face.
    Use your fingertips to gently massage the oil
    into the skin for a minute or two to remove
    impurities like makeup and dead skin cells.
    Leave it for another minute or two and let
    it penetrate the skin.
    4. Use a warm towel to wipe it off.
  3. Brow and Lashes Serum
    Directions:
    Fill up a new mascara tube with NewLife™ Pure Castor
    Oil and apply on lashes and brows before bedtime
    for thicker and longer lashes and brows.
  4. Constipation Relief/Laxative
    Direction:
    Take 15ml or 3 teaspoons of NewLife™ Pure Castor Oil
  5. Nail Care
    Direction:
    Massage your nails and cuticles with a few drops of
    NewLife™ Pure Castor Oil.
