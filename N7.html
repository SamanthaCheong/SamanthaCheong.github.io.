Magnesium

In the metabolism of calcium, vitamin C, phosphorus, sodium, potassium, etc.,
magnesium is a necessary substance and plays an important role in the normal
operation of neuromuscular functions and blood sugar conversion.

Magnesium benefits:

  1. Promote the health of the heart and blood vessels and prevent heart attacks
  2. Prevent calcium from precipitating in tissues and blood vessel walls, preventing
    the occurrence of kidney stones and gallstones
  3. Make teeth healthier
  4. It can help fight depression and can be used as a natural sedative when used
    together with calcium

Magnesium supplementation cycle:

Daily supplementation is recommended.

Signs of excess magnesium:

It can cause motor muscle disorders and hinder the effective utilization of iron
in the body. Excessive magnesium can not only affect the absorption and utilization
of calcium; excessive intake of magnesium can lead to slippery intestines.

Food origin:

Unground grains, figs, almonds, nuts, various seeds, dark leafy greens, bananas, etc.

Need crowd:

People with alcoholism usually suffer from magnesium deficiency. People who drink
frequently are better to consume more magnesium.

Deficiency disorder

Lack of magnesium can cause symptoms such as nervousness, limb tremors, hypoglycemia,
and heart palpitations
