Zinc

Zinc executes the command and supervision of the effective operation of various
functions of the body and the maintenance of enzyme systems and cells. It is the
main substance for the synthesis of proteins. It can direct the contraction of muscles
and help form insulin. It is an important substance that stabilizes the blood state and
maintains the acid-base balance in the body.

Allows the prostate to function properly and is important for the development of
reproductive organs. There is ample evidence that zinc is necessary for the synthesis
of DNA.

Recommended daily intake of zinc:

  1. The recommended daily intake for adults is 12 to 15 mg. Pregnant and breastfeeding
    women need slightly more. Ingesting large amounts of zinc may suppress the body's
    immune function.

Zinc supplementation cycle:

Daily supplementation is recommended

Symptoms of excess zinc:

Zinc poisoning is less likely to occur

Food origin:

Meat, liver, seafood, beer, pumpkin seeds, chestnuts, eggs, dairy products, sesame
seeds, mustard, etc.

Need crowd:

Men should increase the zinc content in their bodies, and patients with prostate disease
should increase their zinc intake; female friends should increase their zinc intake when
they have dysmenorrhea; People who sweat excessively, drinkers, and diabetics should pay
attention to replenishing zinc in the body; When taking large amounts of vitamin B6, you
should also increase the amount of zinc.

Deficiency disorder

Lack of zinc in the body can lead to prostatic hypertrophy, decreased reproductive function,
arteriosclerosis, anemia and other diseases.
