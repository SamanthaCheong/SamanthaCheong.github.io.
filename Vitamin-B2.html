Vitamin B2 (riboflavin)

Vienna vitamin B 2 is a water-soluble vitamin that is easy to digest and
absorb. The amount excreted increases or decreases according to the needs
of the body and the degree of protein loss.

It does not accumulate in the body, so it must always be taken with food
or nutrition. Supplements are required. Unlike B 1, B 2 is resistant to heat,
acid and oxidation.

Vitamin B2 effects:

  1. Promote development and cell regeneration
  2. Promote the normal growth of skin, nails, and hair
  3. Help eliminate inflammation in the mouth, lips, and tongue
  4. Improve vision and reduce eye fatigue
  5. Interact with other substances to help the metabolism of carbohydrates,
    fats, and proteins.
  6. Recommended daily intake of vitamin B2:
  7. The recommended daily intake for adults is 1.2~1.7mg. During pregnancy,
    1.6 mg is required; during lactation, 1.8 mg is required for the first month
    and 1.7 mg for the next 6 months. People who are often stressed should increase
    their intake.

Vitamin B2 supplementation cycle:

A small amount stays inside the human body, so it needs to be replenished daily.

Food origin:

Milk, liver, green leafy vegetables, eggs, fish, milk, etc.

Need crowd:

Women who are taking birth control pills, pregnant, and lactating need more
vitamin B 2; people who do not often eat lean meat and dairy products should
increase vitamin B 2; people who have long-term diet control due to ulcers or
diabetes are more likely to develop The phenomenon of B 2 deficiency; For all
people with mental stress, it is necessary to increase their intake of multivitamins,
and take them together with vitamin B 6, C and niacin for the best effect.

Deficiency disorder:

People who are deficient in vitamin B 2 will suffer from inflammation and dysfunction
of the mouth, lips, skin, and genitals.
