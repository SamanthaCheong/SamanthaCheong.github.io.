Tomato Royale Breakfast Stack

Ingredient Details

Main Ingredients

  1. Whole wheat bread-four slices
  2. tomato-half less
  3. tangerine-2
  4. lettuce leaves-2
  5. purple cabbage leaves-one slice
  6. cucumber-a short section
  7. apple-Small one, half
  8. milk-225g

Additional Ingredients

  1. Cupid Thousand Island Dressing-1.5 spoons

Cooking Method

  1. Milky aroma-taste
  2. other-Craftsmanship
  3. ten minutes-time consuming
  4. Simple-difficulty

Tomato Royale Breakfast Stack

Tomato whole wheat sandwich breakfast ingredients.
First cut the tomatoes into 2 large pieces and save the rest for the
salad. Then spread 2 slices of whole wheat bread with Thousand Island
dressing.
Place tomato slices on bread slices and top with lettuce leaves.
Slice the remaining tomatoes, then shred the purple cabbage and extra
lettuce, then cut the cucumber into 6 thin slices.
Then put Thousand Island dressing on top of the vegetables.
Then mix well and it becomes a vegetable salad.
Then cut the apples into nice shapes with a knife.
Nanfeng Tangerine cuts the outer skin and then wraps the skin to form
an orange flower.
Then peel the kiwi and cut into slices.
Then put all the ingredients on the plate.
Pour the milk into the cup and mix with the sandwich, and this simple
breakfast is ready!
