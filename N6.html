Iodine

2/3 of the iodine in the human body is found in the thyroid gland. The
thyroid gland controls metabolism, and the thyroid gland is affected by
iodine.

Therefore, if there is insufficient iodine, it may cause slow
mental reaction, body weight, and lack of energy.

Functions of iodine:

  1. Transform excess fat and help reduce weight
  2. Promote appropriate development
  3. Give vitality
  4. Improve response agility
  5. Promote the health of hair, nails, skin and teeth.

Iodine supplementation cycle:

Daily supplementation is recommended.

Symptoms of excess iodine:

It causes metabolic disorders in the human body and manifests
itself as weight loss, muscle weakness and other symptoms. Excessive
consumption of iodine can also cause goiter

Food origin:

Kelp and other seaweeds, onions and seafood are vegetables grown in
iodine-rich soil

Need crowd:

People who eat too much raw cabbage vegetables may develop iodine
deficiency; People with severe iodine deficiency will suffer from
thyroid hypertrophy, hypothyroidism and other diseases.

Deficiency disorder

Thyroid hypertrophy, hypothyroidism.
