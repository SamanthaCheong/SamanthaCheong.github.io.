Vitamin B1 (Thiamine)

Vitamin B1, also known as thiamine or anti-neuritis, is a water-soluble
vitamin. Like all B vitamins, excess vitamin B1 is not stored in the body
but is completely excreted. Therefore, it must be replenished every day.

There is a synergistic effect between the B vitamins - that is, taking all
the B vitamins at once is more effective than taking them separately. Also,
there is no effect if the intake ratio of B1, B2, and B6 is uneven (the ratio
is as follows: B1 50mg, B2 50mg, B6 50mg).

Vitamin B1 Effect:

  1. Help digestion, especially the digestion of carbohydrates
  2. Improve mental condition and eliminate fatigue
  3. Maintain normal activities of nerve tissue, muscles, and heart
  4. Reduce motion sickness and seasickness
  5. Treat beriberi
  6. It can relieve the pain after dental surgery
  7. Helps in the treatment of herpes zoster (herpes zoster)
  8. Improve memory
  9. Recommended daily intake of vitamin B1:
  10. The recommended daily intake for adults is 1.0~1.5mg. During pregnancy and
    lactation, take 1.5~1.6mg per day. When you are sick, have a stressful life,
    or undergo surgery, you should increase your intake. Vitamin B1 is called a
    spiritual vitamin because vitamin B1 has a good impact on nervous tissue and
    mental state.

Vitamin B1 supplementation cycle:

Vitamin B1 only stays in the human body for 3 to 6 hours, so it must be
supplemented every day.

Symptoms of excessive vitamin B1 :

Excessive supplementation may cause drowsiness or mild wheezing.

Food origin:

Rice bran, whole wheat, oats, peanuts, pork, tomatoes, eggplants, cabbage,
milk, etc.

Need crowd:

Loss of appetite, gastrointestinal diseases, dry hair, memory loss, and
cramps (muscle spasms) indicate that you may be deficient in vitamin B1;
People who smoke, drink alcohol, and like to eat sugar should increase their
intake of vitamin B1; those who are pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking Women who
take birth control pills need a lot of vitamin B1; if you need to take a gastric
acid suppressant after a meal, you will lose the vitamin B1 you get from this meal;
People who are in a stressful state, such as illness, anxiety, mental shock, After
surgery, etc., not only B1 is needed, but all vitamins in the B family are needed.

Eficiency disorder:

Beriberi
